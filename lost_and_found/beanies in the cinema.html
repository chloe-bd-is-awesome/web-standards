BEANIES IN THE CINEMA

••••••••••••••••

On a cold night, I decided to go to the cinema, bringing my beanie, a coat and a scarf. I watched Shrek 2, sitting right towards the back to get the best view of the screen. Just warmed up and half asleep, the credits rolled in joulting me awake! I stood up abruptly, the lights coming on, my friend already making her way down the steps, i looked to my surrondings, picking up my scarf that was visibly hung on the back of my chair, laying ontop of the seats. I left the cinema eagerly chatting about the movie, before suddenly stopping in my tracks. My red striped beanie was missing, a gift from my mum for my birthday! Distraught, I revisited the cinema immediatley, asking for the lost property bin and demanding to rummage through it myself. The bin was large, with ranom items bike locks, keychains and smaller toys, but after navigating through the mess, i hit the bottom of the bin. No beanie was to be seen. One after another, i kept making this mistake, re-living the same cycle of happiness and then dissapointment leaving beanies each time i visited. 4 beanies lost, 4 times too many.

I recreated the cinemas lost property bin, in the form of a website, so that you too can rummage through the lost items. This website is a growing community, being reunited with a range of personal items from camberwell, that are listed in the lost bucket and discovered in the found bucket. This site also acts as a chat, where people can bond with their shared loss of abstract thoughts and wills. The page commemerates all those beanies and ethat history won't repeate itself.

If you have items to add, dont hestiate to scan the QR code! We hope you can not only find your lost item, but also find a new story.
