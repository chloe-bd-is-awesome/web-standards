WHAT WILL OUR WEBSITE BE?

Our idea is based on the Lost and Found system of sorting, boxing and housing personal items. The organic chaos, sorted at the hand of someone who doesn’t have time to gather and label a wide range of items. Our intention for the website is to create a gallery showing the lost property organised within categories, or everything shown on one page. We want the site to have a chaotic yet personal feel, mimicking that of a room in a house, as well as a rock being thrown into the ocean. We are going to include user experience features that allow the audience to engage with what they have lost through a fun and interactive style of navigating through the site.
